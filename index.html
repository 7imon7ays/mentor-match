How it works

1

You fill out a short form to tell us whether you want to mentor, learn, or botha. You also tell us what topics interest you these days and where you're located. Whether it's object-oriented fundamentals or the dark recesses of systems programming, there's someone out there who wants to nerd out about it too.

2

We email you a few profiles and you tell us who you'd enjoy learning from or teaching. Users are only identified by their initials, their location, and topics of interest. If both sides agree, we make an introduction.

3

You get together for coffee and discuss the topics you agreed on. The mentor gets a refresher on stuff they know but never use at work. The student gets the kind of guidance that's hard to find outside university. Both make a friend.

Rules

  • - No money changes hands.
  • - Mentor Match takes no responsibility for what happens between users.
  • - The only romance allowed is love of code.
  • - You may only participate if you are 18 years old or more.

Sign Up

Submit your email address. You'll receive a short questionnaire and get matched with a mentor or pupil.
